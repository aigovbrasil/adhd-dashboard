# Sources and Safety Boundary

This skill is organizational and workflow-oriented. It does not diagnose ADHD/TDAH, prescribe treatment, or replace clinical care.

## Evidence-aware source anchors

| Source | Relevance |
|---|---|
| NIMH ADHD overview | Defines ADHD as a developmental disorder involving persistent inattention, hyperactivity, and impulsivity. |
| CDC adult ADHD overview | Notes ADHD develops in childhood and may continue into adulthood; adult diagnosis and treatment require professional evaluation. |
| NICE NG87 | Covers recognition, diagnosis, and management of ADHD across children, young people, and adults. |
| CHADD ADHD overview | Provides patient-facing education on ADHD characteristics and persistence. |

## Safe Use

| Safe | Unsafe |
|---|---|
| Workflow planning | Diagnosis |
| Printable dashboards | Medication advice |
| Task initiation support | Treatment recommendations |
| External memory systems | Claims of curing ADHD |
| Organization templates | Replacing clinician assessment |
